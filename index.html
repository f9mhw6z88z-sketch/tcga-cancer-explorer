How this works

Our Logistic Regression model was trained on RNA-seq gene expression data from The Cancer Genome Atlas (TCGA). Each gene was ranked by its ANOVA F-score — measuring how strongly its expression differs between each cancer type and all others. When you enter a gene ID, we show which cancer types that gene most powerfully distinguishes. The higher the F-score, the more diagnostic that gene is for that cancer.
